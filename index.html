About Trident Systems

We're a Canadian water utility company bringing modern digital solutions to critical infrastructure management. Our team combines firefighting experience, NFPA certification, and full-stack development expertise to create tools that work in the field.

From fire flow testing to comprehensive asset management, we help water utilities, fire departments, and municipalities maintain safe, efficient water systems.

Based in Ontario, we serve communities across Canada with certified testing services and cloud-based software solutions.
