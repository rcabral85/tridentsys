Comprehensive Hydrant & Water System Maintenance Services

Beyond certified NFPA 291 fire flow testing, Trident Systems provides full-spectrum hydrant maintenance for municipalities, businesses, and property managers across Southern Ontario. All work follows AWWA, NFPA, and OTM Book 7 standards.

  • Scheduled Hydrant Inspections: Lubrication, cleaning, and functional checks
  • Valve Exercising: Prevents seizing and ensures rapid emergency activation
  • GIS Flagging & Location: Accurate mapping for asset management
  • Leak & Pressure Monitoring: Identifies issues before they escalate
  • Remediation & Repairs: Fixes faulty, stuck, or broken hydrants quickly
  • Emergency Response: 24/7 availability for urgent repairs or incidents
  • Painting & Asset Protection: Keeps equipment easily identifiable and code-compliant
